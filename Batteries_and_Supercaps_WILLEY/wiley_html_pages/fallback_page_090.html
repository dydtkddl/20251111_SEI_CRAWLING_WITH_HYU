Graphical Abstract

The front cover artwork is provided by the group of Prof. Bensch at the inorganic chemistry department of Christian-Albrechts-University Kiel (Germany). The image illustrates the copper – sodium extrusion mechanism in CuCrS2 during the first step of sodium uptake. Additionally, the results of ex-situ X-ray diffraction measurements and pair distribution function measurements are displayed.More information can be found in the article by M. Krengel et al. (DOI: 10.1002/batt.201800039).
